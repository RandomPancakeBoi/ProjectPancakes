Hey, I’m Oliver, the person behind ProjectPancakes.

My goal is simple: to make tech repairs, upgrades, and advice accessible to everyone. Whether you need a device fixed, a custom PC built, or just some help with another tech issue, I’m here for you.

I’ve been passionate about technology for as long as I can remember. Growing up, I was the kid who took apart gadgets just to see how they worked; and that curiosity has stuck with me over the years. That’s what led me to start ProjectPancakes in 2018. Since then, I’ve been offering personal, reliable services from the comfort of my home; and, when needed, in others’ homes too.

What makes ProjectPancakes different is how I treat my customers. Whether you’re a first-time customer or a returning one, I’m always happy to chat, answer questions, and make sure you feel confident about your tech.

I’m here to make the process easy, clear, and stress-free for you.If you need help with tech or just want to talk things through, don’t hesitate to reach out. I’m here to make sure you get the best service, no matter what.
